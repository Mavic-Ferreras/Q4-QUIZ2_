Manila and its History

The Philippines especially in Manila contains numerous historical landmarks from its rich history and I will be showing some of the best in my opinion!

Below are six in my opinion the best historical landmarks that you can find here in Manila:
Q2 Project

Rizal Park

Rizal park is a memorial of Philippine national hero Jose Rizal who died on December 30, 1896 this memorial was built on December 8, 1901 with help from the Americans.

San Agustin Church

The San Agustin Church was built on January 19, 1607 during the Spanish colonial period.

Quiapo Church

The Quiapo Church was built on 1929 it is located in Quiapo.

The National Museum

The National Museum was built on 1941 and it contains many of the national treasures of the Philippines.

Manila Cathedral

The Manila Cathedral was built December 8, 1958 or also known as The Minor Basilica and is also the highest seat of the archbishop in the country.

Manila Baywalk

Manila Baywalk is known for their beautiful sunsets and is the eastern side of the city of Manila.
